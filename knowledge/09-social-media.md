# Social Media Marketing

## Overview

Social media is a leverage channel for solo technical founders building SaaS products. Success requires choosing one platform where your target audience congregates, maintaining consistent publishing (3-5x weekly), and measuring pipeline impact—not vanity metrics. The winning pattern: authentic authority through genuine engagement, value-first content, and patient 6-12 month runway before expecting measurable traction.

## Platform Selection

**Channel-Founder-Market Fit Model**: Choose platforms where (1) your target audience actually congregates AND (2) you genuinely enjoy spending time. Forcing yourself onto platforms you dislike kills consistency.

| Platform | Best For | Posting Cadence | Primary Mechanism |
|---|---|---|---|
| **LinkedIn** | B2B/SaaS, professional services | 3-5x weekly | Founder-led authority, thought leadership |
| **Twitter/X** | Tech founders, developers, B2C | 3-5x weekly + daily engagement | Conversations, community, announcements |
| **TikTok** | B2C, younger demographics, entertainment | Daily (if committed) | Short-form video, trend participation |
| **Reddit** | Dev tools, open-source, niche problems | Minimal posting (comments first) | Community participation, Q&A |
| **Hacker News** | Dev tools, infrastructure, indie products | Weekly "Show HN" or comments | Credibility, high-intent audience |
| **Communities (Discord/Slack)** | Niche, high-intent, existing relationships | Daily participation, minimal selling | Relationship building, support |

**Anti-Pattern**: Building across 5+ channels simultaneously kills consistency. Pick one platform, dominate it for 6-12 months, then expand.

## LinkedIn Playbook

### Profile Optimization

**3-Element Architecture** (treat as landing page):
- **Banner**: Single-sentence value prop including what you do + target audience + social proof
- **Headline**: `[Role] @ [Company] / We help [ICP] achieve [outcome] by [method]`
- **Featured Section**: Case studies, CTAs, lead magnets, top-performing posts

**Anti-Patterns**: Avoid third-person "About" sections, buzzword-heavy taglines ("disruptive AI-powered platform"), AI-generated stock images.

### Content Strategy

**TOFU-MOFU-BOFU Content Model**:
- **TOFU** (Awareness): Broad frameworks, industry observations, actionable tips
- **MOFU** (Consideration): Detailed how-tos, case studies, expertise demonstrations
- **BOFU** (Conversion): Social proof, testimonials, specific use cases
- **Target**: 1x per week in each stage minimum (3 posts/week total)

**Content Creation Process**:
1. Record 30-45 min weekly conversation with cofounder/customer
2. Extract 2-3 key insights/stories from conversation
3. Write 3-5 posts from different angles (framework version, story version, lessons-learned version)
4. Spend 50% of writing time crafting opening hooks (5 lines for text posts, 3 lines for videos/images)

**10 Content Pillars** (rotate through weekly):
- Frameworks/methodologies (your IP)
- Case studies (before/after proof)
- How-tos (tactical tutorials)
- Contrarian takes (thought leadership)
- Mistakes/lessons (relatable, teachable)
- Testimonials (social proof)
- Feature announcements (news)
- Q&A from customers (service)
- Tool lists (curated value)
- Milestones (transparency + motivation)

### Posting Cadence

- **Minimum**: 3 posts per week (less than this kills algorithmic reach)
- **Optimal**: 5 posts per week
- **Timing**: Publish during peak hours (9-11 AM in your timezone)
- **Extended gaps**: Cause audience attrition beyond core customer base (maintain momentum)

### Engagement Tactics

**Daily Engagement Ritual** (20-30 min):
1. Spend 15-20 min leaving thoughtful comments on 5-10 target audience posts
2. Respond to post comments within 30-60 min (boosts organic reach)
3. Maintain 3-5 "amplifier" relationships who comment early on your posts (algorithmic boost)

**The 95/5 Rule**: Build trust long before direct sales. Most prospects aren't buying yet—establish credibility through consistent value delivery.

**Strategic List-Building**:
- Categorize connections: influencers, ICP, customers, cheerleaders, peers, mentors
- Send 200+ personalized connection requests weekly (show genuine relevance)
- Prioritize profile visitors and post engagers (warm signals)

**5-Phase Pipeline**:
1. Connection (targeted outreach)
2. Offer Help (value first)
3. Qualification (understand their problem)
4. Meeting (deeper conversation)
5. Follow-up (systematic nurture)

## Twitter/X Playbook

### Profile Optimization

- **Bio**: Clear, specific positioning (e.g., "Building [product]. Thoughts on [topic].")
- **Pinned Tweet**: Your best-performing post or clearest value prop
- **Links**: Direct to highest-value destination (product, newsletter, demo)

### Content Strategy

**Content Mix**:
- Product announcements and feature updates
- Educational content (frameworks, tutorials, hot takes)
- Entertainment (memes, humor, community celebration)
- Community highlights (user wins, PRs, contributor shoutouts)
- Live event coverage (pre-event teasers, during-event updates, post-summaries)

**Visual-First Approach**: Almost every tweet should have an image or well-formatted thumbnail. Use tools like Carbon or Snappify for code snippets; embed screenshots/GIFs for products.

**Tactical Formats**:
- "Wrong Answers Only" (engagement-driving Q&A)
- Launch week batching (group releases into themed event weeks)
- Meme + announcement combos (increases shareability)
- "Top 10 tools I can't live without" (curator positions)

### Growth Tactics

- **Daily engagement**: 15-20 min commenting on ICP/influencer posts
- **Consistency**: 3-5 high-quality posts per week (quality > quantity)
- **Avoid**: Lengthy threads, generic promotional copy, broad industry commentary
- **Dev-specific**: Get developers to write tweets, not marketing copy (authenticity drives engagement)

**Success benchmark** (Supabase): 28K followers in 2 years, 400+ likes per post weekly—far above "billion-dollar dev company" average (10 likes, 2 retweets).

## Building in Public

### What to Share

- Work-in-progress screenshots and demos
- Business metrics and milestone achievements
- Development, marketing, hiring strategies
- Bug fixes and feature launches
- Industry insights and obstacles overcome
- User feedback and testimonials
- Weekly progress updates
- Learning and failures (show the struggle)

### What NOT to Share

**Transparency Decision Matrix** (Stop being transparent when):
1. Competitors gain unfair data advantage
2. Copycat competitors emerge
3. Public pressure management becomes burdensome
4. Metrics become misleading without context

**Build in Public ≠ Bragging in Public**: Share progress and learnings that help others, not just vanity metrics.

### Cadence

- **Minimum**: Weekly progress updates
- **Optimal**: 3-5 posts per week across platforms
- **Extended silence**: Kills momentum and audience beyond core followers
- **Weekly Standup Format**: Daily accomplishments + next-week priorities

## Social Listening

### Setup

**4-Step Implementation**:
1. Identify channels where target audience congregates
2. Define keywords to track (brand, competitors, pain points, buying signals)
3. Monitor channels (automated or manual calendar blocks)
4. Engage meaningfully with high-intent mentions

### Keywords to Track

- Your brand name + variations
- Product category terms (e.g., "form builder", "e-signature tool")
- 3-5 competitor names
- Pain point phrases your ICP uses
- Buying signals: "recommend", "alternative to", "looking for", "choosing"
- Industry jargon relevant to your space

### Tools

| Tool | Cost | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **F5Bot** | Free–$58/mo | Reddit, HN, Lobsters alerts (essential) |
| **Google Alerts** | Free | Broad web mentions, blogs, news |
| **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** | $60–$99/mo | B2B ICP targeting, profile insights |
| **Octolens** | Paid | B2B SaaS, AI filtering, buy-intent signals |
| **Brand24/Mention** | Paid | General brand monitoring |
| **Gummysearch** | Free–$99/mo | Niche subreddit discovery |

### Response Framework

**Value-First Engagement Sequence**:
1. Contribute genuine value without selling (answers, help, expertise)
2. Build credibility and trust within community first
3. Disclose founder/team affiliation transparently (builds trust on Reddit/HN)
4. Mention your product only when it directly solves a stated problem
5. Move deeper conversations to DMs

**High-Intent Signals**:
- Explicit problem statements: "struggling with X"
- Competitor dissatisfaction: "looking for DocuSign alternative"
- Feature requests or tool comparisons: "vs." searches
- Budget/pricing complaints

**Anti-Patterns**:
- Direct pitching without context (appears spammy)
- Hiding founder affiliation (erodes trust)
- Using unedited AI responses (reads generic)
- Wrong keyword selection (miss opportunities or drown in noise)
- Sporadic checking (miss time-sensitive opportunities)

## Templates

### Daily Engagement Routine (1 hour total)

```
15-20 min: Observation
- Read/observe content in your niche
- Identify 5-10 posts from target audience

15-20 min: Engagement
- Leave thoughtful comments on 5-10 posts
- Ask genuine questions or contribute expertise
- Avoid generic replies ("great", "amazing")

15-20 min: Publishing
- Publish your pre-written content (if on schedule)

10 min: Response
- Reply to comments on your content within 30-60 min
```

### LinkedIn Post Hook Template

**For Text Posts** (5 lines to hook):
```
Line 1: Pattern interrupt or bold claim
Line 2: Context or problem statement
Line 3: Unexpected twist or insight
Line 4: Specific example or data point
Line 5: Curiosity driver or transition to body
```

**For Image/Video Posts** (3 lines):
```
Line 1: Bold claim or question
Line 2: Result or outcome promise
Line 3: Call to scroll/read more
```

### Profile Optimization Checklist

- [ ] Banner: Single-sentence value prop (what + audience + proof)
- [ ] Headline: Role/company + ICP + outcome + method
- [ ] Featured section: 3-5 best resources (CTAs, case studies, demo)
- [ ] About section: First-person, specific positioning (avoid buzzwords)
- [ ] Profile image: Professional, approachable headshot
- [ ] Links: Direct to highest-value destination

## Anti-Patterns

**Critical Mistakes** (All Platforms):
- ❌ Posting less than 3x weekly (kills algorithmic reach and audience attrition)
- ❌ Measuring success by vanity metrics (followers, likes) instead of pipeline and conversions
- ❌ AI-generated or generic engagement ("great post!", "amazing")
- ❌ Selling before establishing community credibility (spam-flagged)
- ❌ Ignoring channel-founder fit (forcing yourself onto platforms you dislike)
- ❌ Delegating founder voice to others (removes authenticity, kills engagement)
- ❌ Posting immediately after account creation (triggers shadowbans, throttling)
- ❌ Building across 5+ channels simultaneously (unsustainable)

**LinkedIn-Specific**:
- Relying solely on company page instead of founder accounts
- Generic, AI-generated comments
- Immediate hard selling in outreach (skip the 95/5 rule)
- Ignoring warm signals (profile visits, engagement)
- Inconsistent founder voice
- No attribution tracking mechanism

**Twitter-Specific**:
- Excessive threading despite popularity
- Generic promotional copy ("Check this out!")
- Chasing viral moments instead of building systematically
- Over-relying on hashtags and trend-jacking
- 5+ daily tweets without engagement returns

**Reddit/HN**:
- Self-promotion and spam (communities punish ruthlessly)
- Hiding founder affiliation
- Wrong subreddit/community selection
- Jumping straight to selling before reputation building

**Universal**:
- Unedited AI responses (reads inauthentic)
- Hidden monetization (erodes trust)
- Extended silence, then reappearing (kills momentum)
- Treating all profiles identically (each should have distinct personality)
